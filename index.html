The Proven Way to Improve Your Speaking and Leadership

Toastmasters is a place where you develop and grow - both personally and professionally. You join a community of learners, who use a hands-on approach and by learning by doing.

Whether you are an executive, a stay-at-home parent, a college student or a retiree, you can benefit.

Toastmasters takeaways:

  • Building skills to express yourself in a variety of situations
  • Preparing and delivering more effective presentations
  • Leading Meetings
  • Speaking off the cuff
  • Handling one-on-one interactions with confidence

How Does it Work?

The environment in a Toastmasters club is friendly and supportive. Everyone at a Toastmasters meeting feels welcome and valued - from guest to beginner to advanced speaker. In a club meeting, you practice delivering prepared speeches, as well as brief, impromptu presentations, known as Table Topics. There is no rush and no pressure: The Toastmasters program allows you to progress at your own pace.
